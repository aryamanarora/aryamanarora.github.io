*ll in Indo-Aryan

Some notes on the development of *ll in Indo-Aryan.
February 21, 2022 · 1 min · 74 words · Me

ṭ ~ ṭh ~ l ~ ll#

  • aṅkōṭa, aṅkōṭha, aṅkōla, aṅkōlla113 ’the small tree Alangium hexapetalum'

l ~ ll#

  • *avala, *avalla819 ‘contrary’
  • avalīyatē, *ullīyatē833 ‘stoops; hides oneself; sticks to’

(r)dr ~ ll#

  • ārdrá, *ālla1340 ‘wet’
  • ārdraka, *āllaka1341 ‘ginger’
    • *āllabhr̥ṣṭa1408 ‘moist crop of maize’
